#+title: normalizer of subalgebra
#+filetags: :definition:
#+last_modified: [2023-05-27 Sat 21:25]
#+setupfile: ~/Documents/org/latex_template.org
Given a [[id:04c2e796-c1db-4e7b-8317-6409312fcba6][Lie algebra]] \( L \) and \( H \subset L \) a subalgebra, the *normalizer* of \( H \) is defined to be \( N_L(H) = \left\{ x\in L \mid [xH] \subset H \right\} \).
