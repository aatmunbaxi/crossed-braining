A *super-modular category* is a [[id:872a86ea-69d4-4056-a632-e61ff242906f][unitary]] [[id:0adf10d9-1ea5-4db2-a8bb-6641fab04ea3][premodular category]] such that every non-trivial transparent simple object is isomorphic to a [[id:737d21d5-5774-46d0-8415-00dac1c3cad6][fermion]] \( f \).
The obstruction to modularity is a nontrivial transparent object--namely the fermion.
See [[id:6df43694-0646-474e-a3ba-0d1280fc775a][Brugieres' condition for modularity]].

Super-modular categories model electron liquids with FQH effect.
