#+title: balancing equation
#+created: [2024-01-25 Thu 14:59]
#+setupfile: ~/Documents/org/latex_template.org
The *balancing equation* relates the [[id:4831a1ae-6098-4781-92af-d03c8e8dcf32][braiding]] of a [[id:02790fa5-0095-4320-b7c8-225f269fb335][ribbon category]] to the *twist* \( \theta \).
That is the equation
\begin{equation}
\theta_{X \otimes Y} = (\theta_X \otimes \theta_Y ) \circ c_{Y,X} \circ c_{X,Y}
\end{equation}
