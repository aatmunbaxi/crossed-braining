#+title: group algebra
#+filetags: :definition:
#+last_modified: [2023-09-03 Sun 12:21]
#+setupfile: ~/Documents/org/latex_template.org
Over a field \( k \), the *group algebra* associated to a group \( G \) is the collection of all formal \( k \)-linear combinations on basis \( G \), with the multiplication being the usual group multiplication.
From associativity of \( G \), the resulting algebra is associative.
