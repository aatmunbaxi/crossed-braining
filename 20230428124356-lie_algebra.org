* Definition
A *Lie algebra* is a vector space \( V \) equipped with an operation \( [-,-] : V \times V \to V \) satisfying:
- Bilinearity
- \( [xx] = 0 \)
- (Jacobi) \( [x[yx]] + [y[zx]] + [z[xy]] =0 \)

Note these imply anticommutivity.
