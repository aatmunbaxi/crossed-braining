#+title: representation of group
#+filetags: :representation-theory:definition:
#+last_modified: [2023-05-10 Wed 14:59]
#+setupfile: ~/Documents/org/latex_template.org

A representation of a group is a homomorphism \( \rho : G \to \operatorname{GL}(V) \) for some vector space \( V \).
Oftentimes \( V \) is taken to be finite dimensional.
A representation of a group roughly assigns a linearized group action on the vector space.
