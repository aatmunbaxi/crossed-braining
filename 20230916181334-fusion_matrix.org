#+title: fusion matrix

Given a [[id:59cc4766-c370-4c41-8541-d40d1c09c13d][fusion category]] \( \mathcal{C} \) with ordered set of simple objects \( \left\{ \mathbf{1} , X_1 , \ldots , X_n \right\} \), define \( N_{i,j}^k = \dim \operatorname*{Hom}(X_k , X_i \otimes  X_j) \).
The matrix \( [N_k]_{i,j} \) for each \( k \) are called *fusion matrices*, with entries called *fusion rules*.
