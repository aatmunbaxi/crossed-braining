The *Muger center* of a [[id:4831a1ae-6098-4781-92af-d03c8e8dcf32][braided category]] is the subcategory of objects \( X \) such that \( c_{X,Y} \circ c_{Y,X} = \operatorname{id}_{X \otimes Y} \) for all \( Y \).
i.e. the Muger center is the subcategory of *transparent objects*.
