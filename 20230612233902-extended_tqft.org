#+title: extended TQFT

A (fully) *extended TQFT* with values in an \( n \)-category \( \mathcal{C} \) is an \( n \)-functor \( Z: \mathsf{Bord}_n(n) \to \mathcal{C} \).

These TQFTs were originally introduced by Lurie to [[id:7b2cb8d2-b8e0-4e86-b41f-5b2049bd09ad][provide a way to compute local data]] of TQFTs and generalize their pictures.

See: [[id:dbdab25c-644a-4c8b-8ad0-cda00143dbf7][topological quantum field theory]] and [[id:1c9d6cc8-8ba2-4c57-9b71-e9a97ffd7146][extended cobordism category]]
