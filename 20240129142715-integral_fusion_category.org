#+title: integral fusion category
#+created: [2024-01-29 Mon 14:27]
#+setupfile: ~/Documents/org/latex_template.org
A [[id:59cc4766-c370-4c41-8541-d40d1c09c13d][fusion category]] is called *integral* if \( \operatorname{FPdim}(X)\in \mathbf{Z} \) for every object \( X\in \mathcal{C} \).

It is called *weakly integral* if only \( \operatorname{FPdim} (\mathcal{C})\in \mathbf{Z} \).


See: [[id:ee62a9d4-e1b1-4bff-b633-0c501960d135][Frobenius-Perron dimension]]
