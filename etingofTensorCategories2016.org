** braided G-crossed fusion category
:PROPERTIES:
:NOTER_PAGE: (270 0.7285450829831933 . 0.4530986806282723)
:END:

A [[id:54106a90-401a-4ec5-a6e4-c4d395c42c08][braided G-crossed fusion category]] is a [[id:4e58988f-1008-4772-973d-6cde1fcc8822][graded category]] with a [[id:701a4e2f-0726-453e-a293-e14c373f94ea][G-action on category]] such that \( g \cdot \mathcal{C}_h \subset \mathcal{C}_{g h g ^{-1}} \) and [[id:c0826e1f-59a0-4a82-adbf-d295d2eee9b8][G-crossed braiding]] with certain compatibilities.
