#+title: squared norm
#+filetags: :fleeting:
#+created: [2023-10-18 Wed 08:30]
#+setupfile: ~/Documents/org/latex_template.org
The *squared norm* of a simple object in a [[id:59cc4766-c370-4c41-8541-d40d1c09c13d][fusion category]] is defined by \( |V|^2 = \operatorname{Tr}_V(a) \left[ \operatorname{Tr}_{V^{*}}((a ^{-1})^{*}) \right] \) for some isomorphism \( a : V \to V^{**} \).

The value of the squared norm will not depend on the choice of \( a \) since \( a \) is uniquely determined up to scale.
The squared norm in a [[id:97d0ff5f-0354-478e-9551-71752410b2c5][pivotal category]] can be taken with respect to the pivotal structure.
