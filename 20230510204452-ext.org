The group \( \operatorname{Ext}^i( M,N ) \) is the \( i \)-th cohomology of the complex \( \operatorname*{Hom}_{R}(P_{\bullet} ,N) \), where \( P_{\bullet} \) is a projective resolution of \( M \).
i.e. \( \operatorname{Ext} \) is the left derived functor of \( \operatorname*{Hom}(-,N) \).
See [[id:58a49504-b4d6-47bc-8261-066ff8373341][derived functor]]
