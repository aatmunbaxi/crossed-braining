#+title: quadratic form
#+filetags: :definition:
#+last_modified: [2023-05-24 Wed 15:17]
#+setupfile: ~/Documents/org/latex_template.org
A /quadratic form/ on a vector space \( V \) over \( k \) is a map \( V \to k \) such that \( q(av) = a^2 q(v) \) for all \( a\in V \), and the function \( q(u+v) -q(u)-q(v) \) is bilinear.
