#+title: antipode
#+filetags: :definition:
#+last_modified: [2023-09-03 Sun 12:26]
#+setupfile: ~/Documents/org/latex_template.org
An *antipode* is a \( k \)-linear map on a [[id:710836b0-bc52-4b76-99c3-da1fd57e0015][bialgebra]] \( H \) \(S: H \to H \) such that
\begin{equation}
\mu \epsilon = \nabla (\operatorname{id} \otimes S) \Delta = \nabla(S \otimes \operatorname{id}) \Delta.
\end{equation}
