#+title: fusion categories with integral simple FPdims are Rep(H) for fd qHopf algebra
#+created: [2024-01-25 Thu 12:07]
#+setupfile: ~/Documents/org/latex_template.org
Let \( \mathcal{C} \) be a fusion category
The following are equivalent:
- \( \operatorname{FPdim}(X) \in \mathbf{Z} \) for all objects \( X \) (see [[id:ee62a9d4-e1b1-4bff-b633-0c501960d135][Frobenius-Perron dimension]])
- \( \mathcal{C} \) is a representation category of a finite dimensional quasi-[[id:ca8cf6a8-55d1-4c2f-816e-dcb04c073682][Hopf algebra]]
