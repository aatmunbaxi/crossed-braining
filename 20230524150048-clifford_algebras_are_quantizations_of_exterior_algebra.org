#+title: Clifford algebras are quantizations of exterior algebra
#+filetags: :algebra:
#+last_modified: [2023-05-24 Wed 15:00]
#+setupfile: ~/Documents/org/latex_template.org
The [[id:fd998f12-5b30-436a-a8db-3db2a8902d52][Clifford algebra]] can be though of as the [[id:b03e7804-1eba-4711-a2f2-99a4eba80037][quantization]] of the exterior algebra.
More precisely if \( Q \) the [[id:9767c2b8-23a6-47f1-a3de-ab1c65690629][quadratic form]] is \( 0 \), then \( \operatorname{Cl}(V,Q) = \bigwedge_{}^{ } V \) as vector spaces.
However, the Clifford multiplication is not the same as \( \wedge \).
