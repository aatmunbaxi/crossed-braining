#+title: pivotal category

A rigid [[id:7fc90d0d-1696-4fcb-88b4-48007536481c][monoidal category]] is said to be *pivotal* if it can be equipped with an monoidal isomorphism of [[id:7fc90d0d-1696-4fcb-88b4-48007536481c][monoidal]] functors \( i : \operatorname{id} \to \phantom{A}^{**} \).
