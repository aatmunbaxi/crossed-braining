A *Hopf algebra* is a [[id:710836b0-bc52-4b76-99c3-da1fd57e0015][bialgebra]] with invertible [[id:9fc4dc45-c3a9-4355-923a-9fb6d812565e][antipode]] \( S \).



Hopf algebras can be motivated by making the study of [[id:0273392f-6ba4-44aa-912f-ff0d5221ae3a][group algebra]] [[id:32ef7b6a-2143-4b73-acf5-e2f2ee309e62][representation theory]] nicer.
