* Definition
The Hilbert scheme is the representing object of the Hilbert functor \( \mathsf{Hilb}_X : \underline{S-Schemes} \to \underline{Set} \), taking an \( S \)-scheme \( T\to S \) to the set of sub[[id:436eeda5-0fcf-4741-b906-fcb50805de8d][scheme]]s \( Z \subset X \times _S T \) such that \( Z \to T \) is flat, proper, and of finite presentation.
