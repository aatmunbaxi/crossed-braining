#+title: Weyl chamber
#+filetags: :fleeting:
#+created: [2023-10-31 Tue 09:52]
#+setupfile: ~/Documents/org/latex_template.org
For a given [[id:b23950d7-a56d-4745-aed3-ceb2f6e79c90][root system]] \( R \), a *Weyl chamber* is a connected component \( \mathbf{E} - \bigcup_{\alpha}^{} P_{\alpha} \) where \( P_{\alpha} \) is the plane spanned by the root \( \alpha \).
