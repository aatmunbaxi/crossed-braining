A *modular category* is a [[id:02790fa5-0095-4320-b7c8-225f269fb335][ribbon]] [[id:59cc4766-c370-4c41-8541-d40d1c09c13d][fusion category]] with nondegenerate \( S \)-matrix \( S_{XY} = \operatorname{Tr}(c_{Y,X}c_{X,Y}) \).

Here \( \operatorname{Tr} \) is the [[id:86239a40-ee69-4ffd-bf07-296c74dff52b][categorical/quantum trace]].
