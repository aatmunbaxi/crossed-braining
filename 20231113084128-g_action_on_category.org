#+title: G-action on category
#+filetags: :definition:
#+created: [2023-11-13 Mon 08:41]
#+setupfile: ~/Documents/org/latex_template.org
Let \( G \) be a group.
A \( G \)-action on a [[id:7fc90d0d-1696-4fcb-88b4-48007536481c][monoidal category]] is a functor \( \operatorname{Cat}(G) \to \operatorname{End}_{\otimes }(\mathcal{C}) \).
Here \( \operatorname{Cat}(G) \) is the category defined by objects being elements of \( G \) and tensor product being the group binary operation.
