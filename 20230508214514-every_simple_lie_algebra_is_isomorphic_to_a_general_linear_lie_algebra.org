#+title: every simple Lie algebra is isomorphic to a linear Lie algebra
#+filetags: :representation-theory:
#+last_modified: [2023-05-08 Mon 21:45]
#+setupfile: ~/Documents/org/latex_template.org
A linear [[id:04c2e796-c1db-4e7b-8317-6409312fcba6][Lie algebra]] is a subalgebra of \( \mathfrak{gl}(V) \) (the matrix algebra) for some vector space \( V \).

The [[id:bb4fb805-cba4-465c-9b64-147d4fbe9416][adjoint representation]] provides an isomorphism from a [[id:24075658-eb53-4eed-ab50-25dc277d5bd6][simple Lie algebra]] and a linear Lie algebra

- This is particularly useful, since this implies we can reduce computations of simple Lie algebras to matrix computations
