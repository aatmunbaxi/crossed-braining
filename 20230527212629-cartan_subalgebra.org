A *Cartan subalgebra* of a [[id:04c2e796-c1db-4e7b-8317-6409312fcba6][Lie algebra]] \( L \) is a [[id:a071ca4e-e692-4487-855f-5dc3c6bf8190][nilpotent]] subalgebra which equals its [[id:693f018d-e761-4044-b7c3-565d3556abae][normalizer]].
