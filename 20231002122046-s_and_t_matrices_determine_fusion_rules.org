#+title: S and T matrices determine fusion rules of modular category
#+filetags: :fleeting:
#+last_modified: [2023-10-02 Mon 12:20]
#+setupfile: ~/Documents/org/latex_template.org
Given knowledge of the \( S \)-matrix of a [[id:0adf10d9-1ea5-4db2-a8bb-6641fab04ea3][modular category]], one can determine the [[id:78f1307e-2b02-4ce9-a696-815d8899577b][fusion rules]] of the category by the *Verlinde formula*
\begin{equation}
\label{eq:1}
N_{i,j}^k = \sum_i^{} \frac{S_{i,t} S_{j,t} S_{k^{*},t}}{D^2 S_{0,t}}
\end{equation}
where \( D = \sum_i^{}d_i^2 \).
