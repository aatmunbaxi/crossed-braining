#+title: properties of Lie algebra ideals
#+filetags: :lie-algebras:
#+last_modified: [2023-08-29 Tue 10:00]
#+setupfile: ~/Documents/org/latex_template.org

Given ideals \( I,J \subset \mathfrak{g}  \) of a [[id:04c2e796-c1db-4e7b-8317-6409312fcba6][Lie algebra]]s, the things you'd hope to be true are true:
- \( [ I,J] \subset \mathfrak{g}  \) is an ideal of \( \mathfrak{g}  \)
  - This is a result of the Jacobi identity
- \( I + J \) is an ideal of \( \mathfrak{g}  \)
- \( I \cap J  \) is an ideal
