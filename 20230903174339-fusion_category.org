A *fusion category* is an abelian, rigid, semisimple, \( k \)-linear [[id:7fc90d0d-1696-4fcb-88b4-48007536481c][monoidal category]] with finitely many simple objects such that \( \mathbf{1} \) is a simple object.

* Examples
- [[id:92e8eb74-fedc-4920-ad97-97b06201a693][Finite dimensional representations of a group]] or [[id:16c17c19-a3fd-42ff-8360-50dad4c722ae][Lie algebra]] or [[id:ca8cf6a8-55d1-4c2f-816e-dcb04c073682][Hopf algebra]]
- The category of \( G \)-graded vector spaces (these classify pointed [[id:4831a1ae-6098-4781-92af-d03c8e8dcf32][braided]] fusion categories via pre-metric groups)
