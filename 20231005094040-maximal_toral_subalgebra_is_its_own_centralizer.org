A maximal [[id:372cdf75-f5eb-4bdc-80d9-2e2e9b2e9e4f][toral subalgebra]] of a finite dimensional /semisimple/ [[id:04c2e796-c1db-4e7b-8317-6409312fcba6][Lie algebra]] over algebraically closed characteristic zero is its own centralizer.
i.e. \( C_{\mathfrak{g} } ( \mathfrak{h} ) = \mathfrak{h}  \).
