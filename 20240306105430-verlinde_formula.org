#+title: Verlinde formula
#+created: [2024-03-06 Wed 10:54]
#+setupfile: ~/Documents/org/latex_template.org

In a [[id:0adf10d9-1ea5-4db2-a8bb-6641fab04ea3][modular category]], the [[id:8079f2ac-766f-4f10-b476-c11379b5f64e][fusion rules]] can be computed with the *Verlinde formula*:

\begin{equation}
N_{i,j}^k = \sum_t^{}  \frac{S_{i,t} S_{j,t} S_{k^{*} ,t}}{D^2 S_{0,t}}
\end{equation}
