#+title: derived subalgebra measures lack of commutivity
#+filetags: :representation-theory:
#+last_modified: [2023-05-08 Mon 21:23]
#+setupfile: ~/Documents/org/latex_template.org
The *derived subalgebra* of a [[id:04c2e796-c1db-4e7b-8317-6409312fcba6][Lie algebra]] \( [LL] \) measures the lack of commutivity of the Lie bracket.
It is evident that \( L \) is abelian if and only if \( [LL]=0 \).
